# 📖 Project Overview

The **TAJ Hotels Business Intelligence Dashboard** is a comprehensive Power BI solution designed to analyze hotel performance across multiple dimensions, including **Revenue, Occupancy, Customer Ratings, Booking Trends, and Room Category Performance**.

This dashboard empowers hotel management with actionable insights to optimize pricing strategies, improve occupancy rates, enhance guest experiences, and maximize overall profitability.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

TAJ Hotels operates multiple luxury and business properties across different cities. Managing performance across these properties presents several challenges:

- Identifying top-performing and underperforming hotels
- Understanding room category contribution to revenue
- Monitoring occupancy patterns and booking behavior
- Tracking customer satisfaction through ratings
- Optimizing pricing and room allocation strategies
- Improving operational efficiency and profitability

To address these challenges, an interactive Power BI dashboard was developed to provide real-time business insights and support strategic decision-making.

### Key Insights
